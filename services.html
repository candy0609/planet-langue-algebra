Online tutoring

Do not worry, for learning at our school, you don't have to be close. We offer online teaching services so that you can learn a new language from the comfort of your home.

Our online tutoring program offers facilities for both children and adults and for the different learning levels. In teaching-learning process, our lecturers are using the latest methodological approaches. This implys interesting and motivating teaching methods and procedures, a variety of teaching tools and aids and only inovative methodical systems.

Book club

You are looking for a new interesting hobbies but do not want to neglect your social life? Join our book club and bring a friend with you, so you can comment on the content of a thrilling novels with coffee and a good company!

The program of our book club contains interesting books of a different style, written in the languages you are learning. After reading a certain book, you can discuss it's content guided by interactive methods, applied by our professional lecturers. Also, you can suggest books from your own reading list and we will make sure to get them involved in the program!

Workshops and seminars

To make the learning process more interesting and dynamic, we also organize various workshops and seminars, considering a particular topic.

Workshops are organized as a team work, while seminar structure depands on your tendencies. That means that you can choose if you would like to do seminar work individully or also in a group. Beside that, it is worth mentioning that there is a certain theme list that you can work on, but you can also create your own theme, related to the program you are attending.
