Catalog

Basic

You chose a language you want to learn but you're worried because you don't have much of a foreknowledge? We have a simple solution for you! You can try out our Basic learning program, in which you get an inovative group tutoring, free work materials, our experienced lecturers, regular feedback and many other benefits. After the completion of the scheduled lessons, you take the exam whose implementation mode is left to your own choice, and you can take a selfie with your C1 certification!

Moderate

If you already have some foreknowledge of a particular language, but still desire to expand your comprehension, this is a perfect level for you! Our Moderate learning program contains an addition of individual tutoring offers, online classes, experienced and professional lecturers and detailed feedback after which you can comfortably pass the exam. And before you know it, here you go, that printed B1 diploma looks great on you!

Advanced

You are someone who is already well introduced to basics of a particular language, so you gain an ambition to spread your wings and fly? Congratulations, here is our Advanced learning program, waiting just for you! Its structure includes everything already listed in Basic and Moderate level, but not just that! You also get flexible hours of a learning process, the highest rated professional lecturers and a paid trip to relevant European country. And don't forget to bring your printed A1 diploma with you!
